KeyLume Studio — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how KeyLume Studio ("we", "us", "our") handles data in connection with our applications, including the KeyLume Automation Pinterest integration and the KeyLume Listing Manager tool.

Data We Collect

KeyLume Studio does not collect, store, or process personal information from any users, buyers, or visitors. Our tools are private, single-user applications used exclusively by the owner of KeyLume Studio.

Third-Party Services

Our applications connect to third-party platforms including Etsy and Pinterest through their official APIs. We access only the data necessary to manage our own shop listings and publish pins for our own art collections. We do not access, collect, or store data belonging to other users of these platforms.

Data Sharing

No information obtained through any API or platform integration is shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties under any circumstances.

Data Storage & Security

API credentials and OAuth tokens are stored securely on private infrastructure. All cached data can be deleted at any time. Revoking API access immediately terminates all data access.

Cookies & Tracking

KeyLume Studio applications do not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any form of user tracking.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at keylumestudio@gmail.com.
